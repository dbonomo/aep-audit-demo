The problem

AEP/CJA drift is quiet, cumulative, and expensive

Every downstream decision — attribution, audiences, exec reporting — inherits the rot. Teams usually find out months later, from a number that "looks off," after the bad data has already shipped.

📉

Schema staleness

Fields get removed or renamed without tracing which dimensions, metrics, and segments depend on them — they silently return null.

🔗

Identity stitching gaps

Person-ID resolution drops below threshold. Journeys fragment. Unique counts inflate. No error is thrown — reports just get quietly wrong.

⏱

Config drift vs. baseline

Timezones, lookback windows, and session timeouts change on data views without annotation — breaking historical trend comparisons overnight.

🚧

Stale pipelines & orphans

Disabled datasets stay attached to data views. Production ingestion pipelines stop feeding. Test sandboxes stay enabled in prod orgs.
